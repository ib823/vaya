The Critical Definition: What IS E-Money?

âš–ï¸ Legal Definition (FSA 2013)

E-money is defined as a payment instrument that:

  1. Stores funds electronically IN EXCHANGE of funds paid to the issuer
  2. Is able to be used as a means of making payment to ANY PERSON other than the issuer
ðŸ’¡ KEY INSIGHT

BOTH conditions must be met. If either condition is NOT met, it is NOT e-money under Malaysian law.

2024 Exemption Orders (Game Changer)

On December 2024, the Minister of Finance issued exemption orders that EXPLICITLY carve out "limited purpose e-money" from BNM regulation:

P.U.(A) 463/2024 Financial Services (Limited Purpose Electronic Money) Order 2024
P.U.(A) 461/2024 Islamic Financial Services (Limited Purpose Electronic Money) Order 2024

What's Exempted: Gift cards, loyalty programs, prepaid mobile credits, closed-loop systems
